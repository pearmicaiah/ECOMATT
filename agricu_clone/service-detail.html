    Crops & Horticulture

    Sustainable Crop Production

    We specialize in high-yield, sustainable crop production tailored to the Zimbabwean climate. From maize to horticulture, our methods ensure food security and quality produce. Agricultural work means the production or initial processing of crops, dairy products, poultry, or livestock, as well as the cultivation or harvesting of trees.

    Agricu Offerings

    • High-yield maize and grain production.
    • Export-quality horticultural produce.

    What's included Agricu service?

    • Soil analysis and preparation
    • Irrigation management
    • Pest and disease control
    Our commitment to sustainable farming practices ensures that we not only meet current food demands but also preserve the land for future generations.

    Food Delivery From Start to Finish

    • First Step: Careful selection of non-GMO seeds adapted to local conditions.
    • Second Step: Precision farming and monitoring during the growth cycle.
    • Third Step: Harvesting at peak maturity for maximum nutrition.
    • Fourth Step: Efficient post-harvest handling and distribution.
    Poultry Farming

    Kwekwe Broilers & Layers

    Our poultry division in Kwekwe is dedicated to raising healthy, hormone-free broilers and layers. We prioritize animal welfare and biosecurity to deliver safe, high-quality poultry products to the market.

    Agricu Offerings

    • Fresh, farm-raised chicken meat.
    • High-quality table eggs.

    What's included Agricu service?

    • Biosecure rearing environments
    • Veterinary health monitoring
    • Nutritionally balanced feed programs
    We utilize modern poultry housing and management systems to ensure the well-being of our flock and the quality of our products.

    Food Delivery From Start to Finish

    • First Step: Hatching & Brooding: Sourcing premium day-old chicks and providing calculated heat and care.
    • Second Step: Feeding & Growth: Utilizing nutrient-rich feed for healthy, steady growth.
    • Third Step: Health Monitoring: Continuous veterinary oversight to prevent disease.
    • Fourth Step: Delivery: Fresh eggs and meat processing and delivery to market.
    Piggery Operations

    Sustainable Piggery Systems

    We implement integrated piggery systems that focus on sustainability and efficiency. Our breeding and rearing programs are designed to produce superior pork products while minimizing environmental impact.

    Agricu Offerings

    • Premium pork cuts.
    • Breeding stock.

    What's included Agricu service?

    • Advanced waste management (biogas)
    • Genetic improvement programs
    • Strict hygiene protocols
    Our piggery operations are integrated with our cropping systems, utilizing manure for fertilizer and ensuring a circular farm economy.

    Food Delivery From Start to Finish

    • First Step: Breeding: Selection of high-genetic stock for superior traits.
    • Second Step: Growth: Monitored growth with veterinary care and optimized diets.
    • Third Step: Processing: Humane and hygienic processing standards.
    • Fourth Step: Distribution: Fresh pork tailored for retail and wholesale.
    Chitsano Roller Meal

    Milling Operations

    Our milling division produces the renowned 'Chitsano Roller Meal', a staple for many families. We combine traditional grains with modern milling technology to deliver nutritious and affordable mealie meal.

    Agricu Offerings

    • Chitsano Roller Meal (5kg, 10kg, 20kg).
    • Maize bran for stockfeed.

    What's included Agricu service?

    • Quality control testing
    • Vitamin verification
    • Bulk supply logistics
    We adhere to national fortification standards to ensure our roller meal contributes to the nutritional well-being of the community.

    Food Delivery From Start to Finish

    • First Step: Grain Selection: Sourcing high-quality local non-GMO maize.
    • Second Step: Milling: Precision grinding for perfect texture and consistency.
    • Third Step: Fortification: Adding essential vitamins and minerals.
    • Fourth Step: Packaging: Hygienic packaging for shelf stability and market distribution.
    Butchery Products

    Farm-to-Table Butchery

    Our butchery provides the final link in our farm-to-table value chain. providing fresh, high-quality meat products directly to consumers. We ensure full traceability and superior freshness.

    Agricu Offerings

    • Beef, Pork, and Poultry cuts.
    • Value-added meat products (sausages, mince).

    What's included Agricu service?

    • Custom cutting services
    • Hygiene certified handling
    • Cold chain assurance
    Experience the difference of meat that comes straight from the source, processed with care and expertise.

    Food Delivery From Start to Finish

    • First Step: Quality Sourcing: Direct from our sustainable farms.
    • Second Step: Cold Chain Storage: Strict temperature management to ensure freshness.
    • Third Step: Professional Cuts: Expert butchery for standard and custom requirements.
    • Fourth Step: Retail: Direct sales and home delivery options.

    Chitsano Roller Meal

    We mill, package, and supply Chitsano Roller Meal produced from quality maize. Our roller meal is available for household use and bulk supply, supporting consistent nutrition for families and livestock across local markets.
