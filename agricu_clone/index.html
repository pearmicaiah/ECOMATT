Get To Know Us

A Trusted Leader in Zimbabwe’s Agriculture and Food Production

Ecomatt Farm is one of Zimbabwe’s fast-growing and most trusted agricultural enterprises, specializing in horticulture farming, livestock production, and value-added food processing. We produce a wide range of fresh vegetables, grains, poultry, pigs, and goat products, supplying wholesalers, retailers, butcheries, and local communities with reliable, high-quality food.
Crops & Horticulture
Specialized fresh vegetables, field crops, and seasonal produce.
Livestock Farming
Sustainable breeding of poultry, piggery, and goats.
Value-Added Products
Processing milling products, butchery items, and goods.
Services & Supply
Providing farm supplies, contract farming, and training.
