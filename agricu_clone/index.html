Ecomatt Farm is a diversified agricultural enterprise based in Kwekwe, Zimbabwe, committed to sustainable food production and responsible farming practices across the region.

Our high-quality piggery and livestock units in Kwekwe utilize modern breeding techniques to supply premium, healthy meat products to local communities and growing markets.

Ecomatt’s dedicated poultry division focuses on efficient, large-scale production, ensuring a steady supply of quality poultry products while maintaining high animal welfare standards.

Specializing in diverse horticulture, our farm produces a wide variety of fresh vegetables and fruits, grown with precision to meet the highest nutritional and quality standards.

Through integrated crop farming, Ecomatt produces essential grains and field crops, driving food security in Zimbabwe through sustainable land management and innovation.

Ecomatt Farm integrates cutting-edge agricultural technology with traditional Zimbabwean wisdom to optimize yields while preserving our precious natural resources for future generations.

Beyond farming, we are proud to empower the Kwekwe community by creating sustainable employment and supporting local supply chains with high-quality, homegrown produce.

From seed to harvest, Ecomatt adheres to rigorous international quality standards, ensuring every product that leaves our farm is safe, nutritious, and of the highest grade.

We are shortening the distance between our fields and your table, delivering peak freshness and flavor through an optimized logistics network across the Midlands province.

Our commitment to the land is unwavering; through crop rotation and organic soil enrichment, Ecomatt Farm is building a resilient and fertile ecosystem in the heart of Zimbabwe.

A Trusted Leader in Zimbabwe’s Agriculture and Food Production

Ecomatt Farm is one of Zimbabwe’s fast-growing and most trusted agricultural enterprises, specializing in horticulture farming, livestock production, and value-added food processing.
