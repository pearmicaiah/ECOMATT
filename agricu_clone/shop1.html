About Ecomatt Farm

Ecomatt Farm is a diversified agricultural enterprise based in Kwekwe, Zimbabwe. We are committed to sustainable farming through crop production, poultry farming, piggery operations, value-added milling, and fresh butchery products. Our focus is on producing quality, affordable food while supporting local markets and communities.
